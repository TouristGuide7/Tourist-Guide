    FAQs

    What people asked?

    Q1: What services does your travel agency provide?

    A: Our travel agency offers a wide range of services including flight and hotel bookings, vacation packages, cruise reservations, car rentals, travel insurance, visa assistance, and personalized itinerary planning.

    Q2: How can I contact your travel agency?

    A: You can reach us through our website's contact form, email, or by phone. Our contact details can be found on the "Contact Us" page of our website.

    Q3: Are you a licensed and accredited travel agency?

    A: Yes, we are a licensed and accredited travel agency compliant with industry regulations. We prioritize the safety and satisfaction of our clients.

    Q4: How can I book a flight through your agency?

    A: Booking a flight is easy! You can either do it online through our website's booking portal or contact our customer support team for assistance.

    Q5: Can I book a hotel without booking a flight?

    A: Absolutely! We offer separate hotel booking options for those who only need accommodations.

    Q6: Do you provide travel insurance?

    A: Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance plans to ensure you're protected against unforeseen events during your trip.

    Q7: How can I make a payment for my booking?

    A: We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards and online payment platforms. The specific options will be provided during the booking process.

    Q8: Are your prices competitive?

    A: Yes, we strive to offer competitive prices for all our services. Our experienced team continuously monitors the market to ensure our rates are fair and reasonable.

    Q9: Are there any hidden fees?

    A: No, we are transparent about our pricing. The total cost, including taxes and fees, will be clearly presented to you before you finalize your booking.

    Q10: Can you help me create a personalized travel itinerary?

    A: Certainly! Our travel experts can work with you to create a customized itinerary based on your preferences, interests, and budget.

    Q11: What if I need to make changes to my itinerary after booking?

    A: We understand that plans can change. Depending on the type of change, there may be associated fees. Contact our customer support team, and we'll assist you with the necessary adjustments.

    Q12: Do I need a visa for international travel?

    A: Visa requirements vary by destination and your nationality. We provide visa assistance services to help you navigate the application process.

    Q13: When will I receive my travel documents?

    A: Your travel documents, including e-tickets and hotel vouchers, will be provided well in advance of your departure.

    Q14: What is your cancellation policy?

    A: Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of booking and the service provider. We'll inform you about the specific policy related to your booking at the time of reservation.

    Q15: How do I request a refund for a canceled booking?

    A: To request a refund, please contact our customer support team. Refund processing times depend on the service provider and payment method.

    Q16: Do you offer group travel packages?

    A: Yes, we offer group travel packages tailored to your group's size, preferences, and budget. Contact us for more information on how we can create a memorable group trip for you.

    Q17: Are there special rates for group bookings?

    A: Yes, we can provide special rates for group bookings, which often come with additional benefits and discounts.
